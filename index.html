It's All About Light


Photography as most of us know it, as a hobby or a vocation, is a private endeavor. We make personal statements with our photographs, wether we realize it or not. The subjects we choose and the way we capture then on camera say a great deal about who we are. Photography has the power to reproduce more than waht wee see, however. It can transform the particular to the universal, the mundane to the poetic. And it is when we add out personal vision to the technology of photography that we cross that line from mere reproduction to an statement.

I am not a photographer and I have all these pictures to prove it.
